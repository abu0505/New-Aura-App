Summary for Developers:
• Fix Needed: Check your Supabase database schema for the table storing FCM tokens. You are missing a UNIQUE constraint on the column used in your "Upsert" logic.
• Observation: The GPUAUX spam and Choreographer frame skips suggest startup performance could be improved, possibly by deferring some plugin initializations.
